## Animation Concept
Beat 1 — THE ENEMY (0-1s): Camera follows behind a man walking through a muddy backyard, he stops and points down at a collapsed corrugated pipe sticking out of the ground. Standing water puddles everywhere. The yard is a mess of failed drainage attempts.
Beat 2 — THE SOLUTION (1-3s): Camera pans from one failed install to the next — the clogged pop-up emitter, the shallow surface drain caked with debris. Then the man turns and walks toward a freshly dug trench where new pipe is being laid properly in gravel at the correct slope angle.
Beat 3 — THE PROOF (3-5s): Quick cut to the same yard during a rainstorm — rain pouring down, but the yard is draining properly. Water flows through the new catch basin, runs through the system, and exits cleanly from a pop-up emitter at the curb. The yard works.
