## Animation Concept
Beat 1 — THE ENEMY (0-1s): Camera holds wide on the chaotic backyard full of botched drainage work by multiple generalists. Standing water, exposed pipe, misplaced sprinkler head, disconnected sump pump. A visual disaster area.
Beat 2 — THE SOLUTION (1-3s): Camera tracks through the yard like a crime scene investigation, pausing briefly on each failed element — the shallow trench with surfacing pipe, the incorrectly placed landscape fabric, the sprinkler-drain conflict, the useless sump pump. Each one a different trade's mistake.
Beat 3 — THE PROOF (3-5s): Camera lifts up and pans to an adjacent section of the same yard where a specialist has begun installing a proper drainage system — a clean deep trench with correct slope, pipe properly bedded in washed gravel, a catch basin at the low point, everything methodical and purposeful. The contrast between chaos and order is the entire story.
