  TESTING NOTES

  - All ads drive to text message CTA
    at (682) 516-6797. This is the
    text-first angle -- the entire
    point is collapsing friction.

  - Primary CTA is always texting a
    photo. Do not swap to form-fill
    or call CTAs on this angle.

  - Proof hooks (H3) require real
    examples. Swap in actual job
    photos and real response times
    before running.

  - Video formats (B) should feel
    like Bert shot them on his phone.
    No studio lighting. No scripts
    that sound scripted. Raw and real.

  - Carousel formats (C) should tell
    the story in order: problem,
    text, reply, fix, CTA. Each slide
    needs to make sense on its own
    for scroll-stoppers.

  - Run H1 and H4 first to test
    direct claim vs. contrarian
    framing. Layer in H2 and H3
    in week two based on results.

  - Track replies per ad using
    unique UTM or ask-on-reply
    ("Which ad did you see?").
