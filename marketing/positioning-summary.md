  ANGLE OPTIONS

──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ①  THE LOCAL DRAINAGE SPECIALIST   ★ primary
     Statement: Azle's only drainage-focused
     contractor -- not a landscaper who added
     drains, not a sprinkler guy who figured
     it out, not a foundation company
     upselling you. We fix water problems.
     That's it.
     Psychology: Being the only specialist in
     a market of generalists creates instant
     credibility and justifies premium pricing.
     Headline: "Azle's only drainage
     contractor. Not a landscaper. Not a
     sprinkler company. We fix water problems.
     That's all we do."
     Best for: Cold traffic, primary brand
     positioning, all ad formats

──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ②  THE CLAY SOIL MECHANISM
     Statement: North Texas clay soil doesn't
     drain like normal dirt -- it swells when
     wet, shrinks when dry, and cracks your
     foundation in between. Generic drainage
     fixes fail here because they're designed
     for normal soil. We build systems
     engineered for Texas clay.
     Psychology: Names the hidden variable
     that explains why other approaches fail.
     Creates an "oh, THAT'S why" moment.
     Headline: "Your drainage keeps failing
     because of Texas clay. Here's how we
     fix it for good."
     Best for: Problem-aware audience,
     homeowners who've had failed installs

──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ③  THE STORM PROOF ANGLE
     Statement: When the next North Texas
     downpour hits, you'll either be watching
     water pool against your foundation or
     watching it flow to the street. We make
     sure it's the second one.
     Psychology: Triggers specific emotional
     memory of watching water rise during
     storms. Natural urgency without
     manufactured timers.
     Headline: "Still watching water pool
     every time it rains? Let's fix that
     before the next one hits."
     Best for: Seasonal campaigns, storm
     season, rain-triggered retargeting

──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ④  THE "TEXT US A PHOTO" ANGLE
     Statement: See standing water? Text us
     a photo. We'll tell you what's causing
     it and what it'll cost to fix -- usually
     within an hour. No sales pitch. No
     appointment needed.
     Psychology: Collapses the buying process
     into one step. Modern communication that
     no competitor offers.
     Headline: "Standing water in your yard?
     Text us a photo. We'll text you back
     with what it'll take to fix it."
     Best for: High-intent leads, mobile
     users, conversion-focused campaigns

──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ⑤  THE "WE FIX WHAT THEY COULDN'T"
     Statement: Half the drainage jobs we do
     are fixing someone else's failed install.
     Landscapers, sprinkler guys, DIY YouTube
     projects -- they all make the same
     mistakes in North Texas clay soil. We do
     it right the first time so you don't pay
     twice.
     Psychology: Attacks the most common
     alternative (hiring a generalist) by
     naming consequences. Targets higher-
     intent customers who already spent money.
     Headline: "Tired of drainage 'solutions'
     that flood again next month? We fix what
     they couldn't."
     Best for: Retargeting, higher-intent
     audiences, competitive positioning

  Why ★ The Local Drainage Specialist:
  At late Stage 2, the market is ripe for a
  specialist to emerge. Nobody in the Azle
  micro-market has claimed the "only drainage
  company" position. Every competitor is a
  generalist. This angle is the foundation
  that all other angles build on -- you can
  run clay soil mechanism ads and storm proof
  ads, but the underlying brand position is
  always "Azle's drainage specialist." The
  other 4 angles are campaign-level hooks
  that reinforce this core identity.
