  2. Once winning hook is found, test
     formats (A vs B vs C) using the
     winning hook.
  3. Scale winning combination.

  likely win for this angle because the
  "stop calling your landscaper" message
  is the most disruptive and shareable.
  H3 (Proof) is the safest bet for
  consistent performance.

  Budget allocation suggestion:
  Phase 1 (hook test): Equal spend
  across 4 video ads for 5-7 days.
  Phase 2 (format test): Equal spend
  across 3 formats for 5-7 days.
  Phase 3 (scale): 80% to winner,
  20% to second place.

  Key metrics to watch:
  - Hook rate (3-second video views
    divided by impressions)
  - CTR (link clicks divided by
    impressions)
  - Cost per lead (form submissions
    and text messages)
  - Lead quality (booked calls vs
    total leads)

  - Homeowners in Parker, Tarrant,
    Wise, and Denton Counties
  - 30-65 age range
  - Home improvement interests
  - Exclude renters if possible
  - Consider lookalike from existing
    customer list
