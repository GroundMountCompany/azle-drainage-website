A few people have told me they haven't booked an estimate because they weren't sure what to expect. Fair enough. Let me walk you through it.

I show up. I'm in a work truck, not a suit. I'll be on time. I'll text you when I'm on my way.

We walk your yard together. I want you there, because I'm going to show you what I see. Where the water collects. Where the slope is wrong. Where the soil is saturated. I'll point things out as we go.

I check the slope. I bring a level. We look at how the ground slopes around your foundation. This tells me where water is flowing — and where it should be flowing instead.

I look at the soil. Clay content varies even within the same yard. I check what we're working with so I can design the system around it.

I check your downspouts. Where are they dumping? How far from the foundation? Are they connected to anything underground?

I explain your options. Not one option that happens to be the most expensive one. Multiple options if they exist. "You could do X now and add Y later" is something I say a lot.

I give you a written quote. Real numbers. On paper (or email). Before I leave your property. No "I'll send you something next week." You get the number that day.

Then you decide. No pressure. No follow-up sales calls. No "this price is only good for 48 hours." The price is the price. Take a week. Take a month. It's your call.

The whole thing takes about 30 minutes. Free. No obligation.
