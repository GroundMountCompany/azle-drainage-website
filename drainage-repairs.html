Drainage Repairs in North Texas

North Texas conditions accelerate drainage system wear and create unique repair challenges. Our clay soil, weather extremes, and environmental factors require specialized repair knowledge and techniques.

Why Drainage Systems Fail in North Texas

North Texas clay soil shifts dramatically, crushing pipes, disconnecting joints, and causing systems to settle unevenly. Our intense rainfall events overwhelm undersized systems. Tree roots aggressively seek water, infiltrating and clogging drainage pipes. Extreme temperature swings expand and contract materials, creating cracks. Sediment from clay soil clogs gravel and pipes faster than in other regions. Understanding these local failure modes allows us to make repairs that last.

Common North Texas Drainage Problems

French drain clogs from clay sediment are extremely common‚Äîthe gravel becomes compacted with clay particles, preventing water flow. Pipes crushed by soil movement require excavation and replacement. Catch basin sumps fill with sediment and require cleaning. Downspout connections disconnect during freeze-thaw cycles. Erosion undermines drainage channels during heavy storms. We've seen and repaired all these issues countless times in our service area.

Preventive Maintenance Extends System Life

Regular maintenance dramatically extends drainage system life in North Texas. Annual inspections catch problems early when repairs are minor. Periodic cleaning removes sediment before it causes clogs. Grate replacement prevents structural failure. Minor repairs prevent major system failures. We offer maintenance programs that save property owners thousands by preventing premature system replacement.

When to Repair vs. Replace

We're honest about repair vs. replacement decisions. Repairs make sense when damage is localized, the system is under 15 years old, or the cost is under 40% of replacement cost. Replacement is better when multiple components have failed, the system is undersized for your property, it's over 20 years old with multiple issues, or repairs would cost more than 60% of replacement. We always provide both options with transparent pricing.
